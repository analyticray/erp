Odoo Backend Integration with Ipay99 Payment Gateway

iPay88 is a leading and award-winning regional Payment Gateway Provider in South East Asia that allow merchants to accept payments online using debit and credit card from almost all major banks and financial institutions in South East Asia (e.g.,, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Bangladesh, Cambodia, and Thailand).

Features

Merchant

  1. iPay88 payment gateway integration with the Odoo Website module.
  2. Any product added in the Odoo Sales/Purchase module can be added/removed to the Odoo website.
  3. Every purchased product by the customer can be tracked at the Sales Order of the Sales module.
  4. Email notification will be sent for every purchased order by the customer. Merchant can keep track of each transaction by the order number.

Customers

  1. Customer can view all products from the Website and he/she can make online purchase WITHOUT the need to sign in. If the administrator requires the customer to sign in before he/she can purchase, this is also possible by creating an account for each customer.
  2. When the customer completes the online order from the website, he/she will be given a choice to make debit/credit payment via iPay88.
  3. The order number, total amount and descriptions will be forwarded to the iPay88 payment gateway, where the customer can fill in his/her credit/debit card detail.
  4. Upon successful payment, the customer can see the "successful confirmation" message on the website.
  5. Customer receives confirmation order in his email inbox.

Installation and Configuration Instructions

  1. Navigate to the Settings menu. From the Settings page, Click on the Browse Apps button, and search for the "Ipay88 Payment Gateway" module. Click on Install button to install the module.
  2. After successfully installed the module, you will need to configure iPay88 Merchant settings. Select Website Admin -> Payment Acquirers. Then search for 'ipay88' to find the setting configuration. Once found, Click on the Configure button.
  3. You will need to enter the required credentials, namely, the Merchant Code and Merchant Key. Normally, this information is provided to you by iPay88. Next, you will need to click on the Publish on Website button and Test Environment. This will activate the iPay88 to be used on the Odoo Website as a production environment. Click Save to finalize.
  4. Once configured, you may add the product that you want to sell on the Odoo Website. As required by the iPay88, it is mandatory to fill in the info on a) product name, and b) product descriptions.
  5. To write Product description, Select the Notes table, and write it in the Description for Quotations field. Click Save once done.
  6. Click on the Publish on Website button to make your product appear on the Odoo Website.
  7. Your Product is now ready to sell online.
  8. Your customer can view the product online by Clicking on the Website menu -> Shop menu. From the Products page, the customer can select the quantity, and adds the product to the shopping Cart.
  9. Your customer will need to fill in their information into this form for Shipping and Billing process. The yellow colour on the text bar indicates that these are compulsory and need to be fulfilled.
  10. Then, they will need to confirm about their orders to proceed. Your customer will be shown a display about the billing, and shipping addresses as well as the price amount of his/her order.
  11. After conforming in the Shipping & Billing process, the system will make an enquiry about payment method. The customer chooses a payment method that is available to proceed and pay the purchased product.
  12. The customer will be directed to the iPay88 website where he/she need to enter his/her payment information i.e., debit/credit card details. Then, the customer need to click on 'Proceed' button to continue.
  13. This system will directly connect the customer tohis/her bank to proceed the requested transaction.
  14. Now, after the customer confirm the purchase and transaction from the bank, the customer will be notifiedon the confirmation of the order.
  15. The customer will also receive a notification on email about his/her order, which will allow him/her to save the information regarding the purchase.
  16. Here is the example of the receipt provided for your customer. The receipt contains information of the company address, shipping address, the product name, quantity, unit price, taxes, as well as the total amount of the price.
  17. After your payment has been made, the iPay88 will notify you about the transaction details for the order.
